# ForgeLog Privacy Policy

_Last updated: 2026-07-09_

ForgeLog is a workout tracking app. This policy covers the ForgeLog mobile app and its companion Wear OS app.

## Data collection

ForgeLog does not collect, transmit, or store any data on remote servers. There is no account, login, analytics, or crash reporting of any kind.

All workout, routine, and exercise data you enter is stored locally on your device in a local database.

## Data sharing between your devices

If you use the Wear OS companion app, your routine and exercise data is synced directly between your phone and your watch using Android's on-device Wear Data Layer API. This sync happens locally between your own paired devices and is never sent to ForgeLog or any third party.

## Third parties

ForgeLog does not share any data with third parties, because it does not collect or transmit any data in the first place.

## Permissions

The Wear OS app requests the vibration permission, used only for local haptic feedback (e.g. rest-timer countdown, personal record alerts). This permission does not involve any data collection.

## Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted here with a new "Last updated" date.

## Contact

For questions about this policy, open an issue at [github.com/forgelog/forgelog/issues](https://github.com/forgelog/forgelog/issues).
